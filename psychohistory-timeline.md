
# Psychohistory Timeline: Asimov's Robots & Foundation Universe


<h2 style="color:#1e90ff;">Robot Era</h2>

- **1949 (1949 C.E.)** Joseph Schwartz disappears from Earth. **Source:** [*Pebble in the Sky* (1950)](https://www.amazon.com/dp/0765319175)
- **1982 (1982 C.E.)** Susan Calvin born; U.S. Robots founded. **Source:** [*I, Robot* (1950)](https://www.amazon.com/dp/055338256X)
- **2007 (2007 C.E.)** Susan Calvin joins U.S. Robots. **Source:** [*I, Robot* (1950)](https://www.amazon.com/dp/055338256X)
- **2064 (2064 C.E.)** Death of Susan Calvin; colonization begins. **Source:** [*I, Robot* (1950)](https://www.amazon.com/dp/055338256X), [*Robots and Empire* (1985)](https://www.amazon.com/dp/0553587579)
- **3200 (3200 C.E.)** Solaria settled; R. Daneel Olivaw created. **Source:** [*Robots and Empire* (1985)](https://www.amazon.com/dp/0553587579)
- **3500 (3500 C.E.)** Daneel Olivaw & Elijah Bailey solve crime. **Source:** [*The Caves of Steel* (1954)](https://www.amazon.com/dp/0553293400)
- **3501 (3501 C.E.)** Bailey & Daneel investigate on Solaria. **Source:** [*The Naked Sun* (1957)](https://www.amazon.com/dp/0553293397)
- **3503 (3503 C.E.)** Bailey, Daneel, Giskard on Aurora. **Source:** [*The Robots of Dawn* (1983)](https://www.amazon.com/dp/0553381660)
- **3505 (3505 C.E.)** Second wave of emigration; BaleyWorld founded. **Source:** [*Robots and Empire* (1985)](https://www.amazon.com/dp/0553587579)
- **3537 (3537 C.E.)** Death of Elijah Bailey. **Source:** [*Robots and Empire* (1985)](https://www.amazon.com/dp/0553587579)
- **3697 (3697 C.E.)** Amadiro plants nuclear amplifiers. **Source:** [*Robots and Empire* (1985)](https://www.amazon.com/dp/0553587579)
- **3700 (3700 C.E.)** Death of Fastolfe; Zeroth Law; Earth made radioactive; Diaspora begins. **Source:** [*Robots and Empire* (1985)](https://www.amazon.com/dp/0553587579)


<h2 style="color:#228B22;">Empire Era</h2>

- **11300 (11300 C.E.)** Rhodia & Nebular Kingdoms free; Encoding Laws. **Source:** [*The Stars, Like Dust* (1951)](https://www.amazon.com/dp/0765319191), [*The Currents of Space* (1952)](https://www.amazon.com/dp/0765319183)
- **12000 (12000 C.E.)** Trantorian Republic → Empire. **Source:** [*The Currents of Space* (1952)](https://www.amazon.com/dp/0765319183), [*Robots and Empire* (1985)](https://www.amazon.com/dp/0553587579)
- **12300 (12300 C.E.)** Half Galaxy in Trantorian Empire. **Source:** [*The Stars, Like Dust* (1951)](https://www.amazon.com/dp/0765319191)
- **12500 (12500 C.E. / 1 G.E.)** Galactic Empire founded; Galactic Calendar. **Source:** [*The Stars, Like Dust* (1951)](https://www.amazon.com/dp/0765319191), [*Pebble in the Sky* (1950)](https://www.amazon.com/dp/0765319175)
