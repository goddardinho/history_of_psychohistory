# Psychohistory Timeline: Asimov's Robots & Foundation Universe

## Robot Era

- **1949 C.E**: Joseph Schwartz disappears from Earth (time travel accident, Pebble in the Sky).
- **1982 C.E**: Susan Calvin born on Earth. U.S. Robots and Mechanical Men incorporated.
- **2007 C.E**: Susan Calvin begins work at U.S. Robots; later becomes chief robopsychologist.
- **2064 C.E**: Death of Susan Calvin. Humanity begins colonizing planets including Aurora. Earth citizens cluster underground, ban robots from cities.
- **3200 C.E**: Solaria, last Space World, settled. Han Fastolfe creates R. Daneel Olivaw, the humaniform robot.
- **3500 C.E**: R. Daneel Olivaw assigned to work with detective Elijah Bailey in New York City (The Caves of Steel).
- **3501 C.E**: Elijah Bailey and Daneel investigate on Solaria (The Naked Sun).
- **3503 C.E**: Bailey investigates on Aurora with Daneel and Giskard Relentlov (The Robots of Dawn).
- **3505 C.E**: Second wave of emigration from Earth begins, led by Ben Baley. First Settler planet named BaleyWorld.
- **3537 C.E**: Death of Elijah Bailey on BaleyWorld.
- **3697 C.E**: Keldon Amadiro and Levular Manamus begin planting nuclear amplifiers on Earth.
- **3700 C.E**: Death of Han Fastolfe. Population of Solaria vanishes. Daneel and Giskard formulate the Zeroth Law of Robotics. Amadiro makes Earth radioactive; Giskard dies. The Great Diaspora begins. Robotic civil war between Giskardians and Calvinians.

## Empire Era

- **11300 C.E**: Planet Rhodia and Nebular Kingdoms shake off Tyrann's rule. R. Daneel Olivaw formulates Encoding Laws for AI.
- **12000 C.E**: Trantorian Republic becomes Trantorian Empire, guided by Daneel Olivaw.
- **12300 C.E**: Half the Galaxy part of Trantorian Empire.
- **12500 C.E / 1 G.E**: Trantorian Empire becomes Galactic Empire. Start of Galactic Calendar.

## Foundation Era

- **827 G.E**: Joseph Schwartz arrives in the future (Pebble in the Sky).
- **900 G.E**: Forced evacuation of Earth; colony established on Alpha.
- **11988 G.E**: Birth of Hari Seldon and Cleon I. Daneel Olivaw begins genetic experiments leading to Seldon's genius and mentalics.
- **12010 G.E**: Cleon I becomes Emperor.
- **12020 G.E**: Hari Seldon lectures on psychohistory; begins developing the science with Yugo Amaryl. Dors Venabili becomes Seldon's wife; they adopt Raych.
- **12040 G.E**: Birth of Wanda Seldon, daughter of Raych.
- **12052 G.E**: Birth of Bellis Seldon. Hari discovers Wanda's mental abilities.
- **12067 G.E / 1 F.E**: Hari Seldon exiled to Terminus; Foundation Calendar begins.
- **3 F.E**: Death of Hari Seldon.
- **50 F.E**: Anacreon declares independence; Seldon's hologram explains Foundation's true purpose.
- **195 F.E**: General Bel Riose campaigns against Foundation; fleet recalled as predicted by psychohistory.
- **260 F.E**: Dagobert VIII rules; Gilmer's troops sack Trantor; Second Foundation signs peace treaty.
- **300 F.E**: The Mule conquers Foundation, disrupting the Seldon Plan.
- **305 F.E**: Second Foundation alters the Mule's mind; he ceases conquest.
- **310 F.E**: Death of the Mule.
- **376 F.E**: Foundation searches for Second Foundation; Gaia overmind unleashed by Daneel Olivaw.
- **498 F.E**: Gaia overmind offers Golan Trevize a choice; Trevize chooses Galaxia (Foundation's Edge).
- **499 F.E**: Trevize locates Earth, meets R. Daneel Olivaw (Foundation and Earth).
