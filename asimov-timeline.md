
# Isaac Asimov's Science Fiction Timeline (Fictional Chronological Order)

This timeline presents the major works of Isaac Asimov and authorized continuations by other authors, ordered by the internal chronology of the universe (not publication date).

---

- [**Psychohistorical Crisis** by Donald Kingsbury (2001)](https://www.amazon.com/dp/0765300346) — Unofficial, but set in a Foundation-like universe and often referenced by fans.

---

### Notes

- The Robot, Empire, and Foundation series are loosely connected, forming a future history spanning thousands of years.
- "I, Robot" is a collection of short stories introducing the Three Laws of Robotics.
- "Robots and Empire" bridges the Robot and Empire series.
- "Foundation and Earth" and the prequels tie together the entire chronology.
- The Second Foundation Trilogy is authorized by the Asimov estate and expands on the prequel era.
- "Psychohistorical Crisis" is not officially part of the canon but is widely regarded as a spiritual successor.
