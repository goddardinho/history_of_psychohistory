# Isaac Asimov's Science Fiction Timeline

This timeline covers Isaac Asimov's major science fiction works, from his robot-related short stories and novels through his last Foundation novel. Chronological order is based on publication date and internal chronology where relevant.

---

- **"Runaround"** (Short Story, 1942)
- [**I, Robot** (Short Story Collection, 1950)](https://www.amazon.com/dp/055338256X)

---

### Notes

- The Robot, Empire, and Foundation series are loosely connected, forming a future history spanning thousands of years.
- "I, Robot" is a collection of short stories introducing the Three Laws of Robotics.
- "Robots and Empire" bridges the Robot and Empire series.
- "Foundation and Earth" and the prequels tie together the entire chronology.
