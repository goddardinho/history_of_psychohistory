
# Key Characters

Major and recurring characters in Asimov's Robots, Empire, and Foundation universe.

## Robot Era

- **Susan Calvin**: Robopsychologist, central to "I, Robot".
- **Elijah Bailey**: Earth detective, featured in Robot novels.
- **R. Daneel Olivaw**: Humaniform robot, appears in Robot, Empire, and Foundation series.
- **Giskard**: Robot with telepathic abilities.
- **Fastolfe**: Spacer roboticist and leader.
- **Amadiro**: Spacer antagonist.

## Empire Era

- **Joseph Schwartz**: Main character in "Pebble in the Sky".
- **Biron Farrill**: Protagonist in "The Stars, Like Dust".
- **Artagail**: Key figure in "The Currents of Space".

## Foundation Era

- **Hari Seldon**: Founder of psychohistory.
- **Cleon I**: Emperor during Seldon's time.
- **Wanda Seldon, Bellis Seldon**: Descendants of Hari Seldon.
- **The Mule**: Mutant conqueror.
- **Trevize**: Key figure in "Foundation's Edge" and "Foundation and Earth".
- **Dors Venabili, Yugo Amaryl**: Important prequel characters.
